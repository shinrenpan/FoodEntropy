中文

一句話總結：食熵不蒐集、不上傳、不分享你的個人資料。你的食材紀錄與照片都存在你的裝置上；若你自行開啟 iCloud 同步，資料會備份到你自己的私有 iCloud（由 Apple 保管），開發者無法存取。

我們蒐集哪些資料

食熵沒有帳號系統、沒有自建伺服器、沒有第三方分析工具。你在 App 內輸入的內容僅供 App 功能使用：

  • 食材資料（名稱、購買日期、到期日期）：儲存於你的裝置本機。
  • 食材照片（相機拍攝或相簿選取）：壓縮後儲存於本機，僅用於在清單上辨識食材。
  • 通知：到期提醒為 iOS 本機通知（Local Notification），不會傳送任何資料到外部。

iCloud 同步（選用）

iCloud 同步預設關閉。若你主動開啟，App 會透過 Apple 的 CloudKit 將你的食材資料備份到你自己的私有 iCloud 帳戶。此資料由 Apple 依其條款保管，開發者無法存取。你可隨時在設定中關閉。

我們不做的事

  • 不要求註冊或登入帳號。
  • 不將你的資料傳送到開發者的伺服器（我們沒有伺服器）。
  • 不使用第三方分析 / 追蹤 SDK。
  • 目前版本不含廣告、不進行廣告追蹤。
  • 不販售或分享你的個人資料。

權限用途

  • 相機 / 相簿：僅用於為食材新增照片。
  • 通知：僅用於到期提醒；可於系統設定隨時關閉。

兒童

本 App 適合一般使用者，不刻意蒐集任何年齡層的個人資料。

政策變更

未來若新增功能（例如廣告），本政策會隨之更新，並於此頁標註更新日期。

English

In one sentence: FoodEntropy does not collect, upload, or share your personal data. Your food records and photos stay on your device; if you turn on iCloud sync yourself, they are backed up to your own private iCloud (managed by Apple), which the developer cannot access.

What we collect

FoodEntropy has no account system, no server of our own, and no third-party analytics. Anything you enter is used solely for the app's features:

  • Food data (name, purchase date, expiry date): stored locally on your device.
  • Food photos (from camera or photo library): compressed and stored locally, used only to identify items in your list.
  • Notifications: expiry reminders are local iOS notifications; no data is sent anywhere.

iCloud sync (optional)

iCloud sync is off by default. If you turn it on, the app uses Apple's CloudKit to back up your food data to your own private iCloud account. That data is held by Apple under its terms and is not accessible to the developer. You can turn it off anytime in Settings.

What we don't do

  • No sign-up or login required.
  • No sending your data to any developer-run server (we don't have one).
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • The current version contains no ads and does no ad tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data.

Permissions

  • Camera / Photos: only to add photos to food items.
  • Notifications: only for expiry reminders; can be turned off anytime in system settings.

Children

This app is for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If future features (such as ads) are added, this policy will be updated and the date above revised.
